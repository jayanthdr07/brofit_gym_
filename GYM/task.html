Weekly Training Plan: BROFIT Challenge

Day Focus Area Specific Tasks / Exercises Duration / Sets
Monday Chest & Triceps Bench Press, Dumbbell Flys, Cable Crossovers, Tricep Pushdowns 4 Sets of 10-12 Reps
Tuesday Legs & Abs Squats, Leg Press, Lunges,
Plank (3x60s), Crunches		 4 Sets of 8-15 Reps
Wednesday 30 minutes light cardio or swimming, stretching, foam rolling (Active Recovery).
Thursday Back & Biceps Deadlifts, Pull-ups (or Lat Pulldown), Barbell Rows,
Dumbbell Curls, Hammer Curls		 4 Sets of 8-10 Reps
Friday Shoulders & Upper Abs Overhead Press, Lateral Raises, Face Pulls,
Hanging Leg Raises		 3 Sets of 12-15 Reps
Saturday Full Body Circuit Kettlebell Swings, Box Jumps, Medicine Ball Slams 4 Rounds of 10 Reps each
Sunday Complete rest and nutrition focus. Prepare for the next week!
